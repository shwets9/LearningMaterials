Snowball
==================
Snowball is a petabyte-scale data transport solution that uses devices designed to be secure 
to transfer large amounts of data into and out of the AWS Cloud.

OLD Scale
-------------
Can migrate upto 80TB of data. More than that size , choose Snowedge 
and when Petabytes of data is concerned, choose Snowmobile. 

Three types: 
	1.Snowball
	2.Snowedge
	3.Snowmobile

All 3 are compute models now, they are not only storage devices. There are instances running inside.

===============
